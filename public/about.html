Our Story

Crab Grabber was founded with a simple mission: bring fresh, bold, and unforgettable seafood experiences to our community. Inspired by coastal seafood markets and Cajun boil traditions, we focus on quality ingredients, authentic flavors, and a welcoming atmosphere.

Freshness First

We source seafood daily to ensure peak freshness and flavor. Our live tanks and careful handling guarantee the highest quality crab, shrimp, lobster, and shellfish available.

Authentic Cajun Experience

Our house blend seasoning combines traditional Cajun spices with our signature flavors, creating a bold and unforgettable taste. Every boil is customizable to your preferred spice level.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a place where friends and family gather to enjoy fresh seafood, celebrate special moments, and create lasting memories. We believe food should bring people together.
